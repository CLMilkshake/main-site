Tournament Info

Put as simple as possible: 5 Teams of 2 Players will participate in 8 versions of minigames I've made myself to show off their skills and have fun!
To get more into the weeds, I have spent the past ~3 years upgrading this tournament and putting what I've learned about coding, logic and even some real life into it, so it's been a lot of small bits of work over long stretches of time to get here. It started out pretty scrappy, but now I am very proud of what I've been able to create, even though it's not completely perfect. I even made my own sounds / songs for this year, and that's turned out great so far!
I have re-created 8 Minigames for my friends (and maybe me?) to participate in:

Parkour

Get as far as you can through many different types of obstacles. Jump over the void, swim through water, and slide down honey to accomplish it! There are checkpoints every once in a while, so if they fall, they won't have to restart at the end

Water Dropper

Drop to the water below without hitting anything else on your way down. It's really that simple!

Super Sumo

Try to knock the other teams out of the ice rink. The more you get hit, the further you'll be knocked back! Each player will have 3 lives before being permanently out

Capture The Point

This one is a special one. Me and a few of my friends hand-made this entire game from scratch. Each player will choose a kit with some sort of advantage, then with their team, they need to capture and hold the point in the center of the map until they capture it. We can only have two teams going at a time, so teams will take turns and all get to play 3 rounds of this game.

Hole in the Wall

It's exactly what it sounds like. Walls will come from a random side, and players have to move quickly to fit themselves into the holes inside said wall, otherwise they might get knocked off the platform.

Spleef / TNT Run

Players will try to break the floor to cause other people to fall by either using a shovel on snow, or walking over the sand. Last team standing wins!

Boat Racing

Players will each hop in a boat and glide across slippery ice to try to be the first to cross the finish line. Boating on ice in Minecraft is pretty chaotic, so this will be fun to watch

Bingo

Players will have a set amount of time to complete as many objectives off a bingo board as they can. I can't reveal what those objectives are yet, but some are pretty whacky. (Fall exactly 12 Meters is one of them 🤫)

And those are the games! There might end up being a final two game, but we'll see if I have time to add that. As the tournament progresses, the games will have multipliers added to their points to make comebacks more likely. The players will decide which games they want to play first and last depending on how confident they are in a certain game, and how well they can "convince" each other to pick the game they want. You'll just have to wait and see what I mean. I hope you enjoy the show at least for a little bit! I know video games aren't for everyone, but I've really tried to make this a spectacle for everyone to watch for at least a short time. If you have any questions, let me know either on the stream chat or any of the ways you can contact me! I'm more than happy to talk about it.
