THE BOARD

Welcome back to The Board! Our free to watch game show is coming back this year! Come see 4 contestants answer trivia, participate in physical challenges, and improv on stage for your votes! This started as a backyard show, and it's grown into an incredible small event, but I still have big dreams for it. I won't spoil those yet though, you'll have to come to the show to hear about them. I'll tell you this much, to reach these dreams, we need to know we can build an audience.
This isn't about making money. The sponsors we have either contribute to the giveaways or cover some of the costs to run the show, and admission is free. It's all about the contestants and you having fun.
You can RSVP down below, and when you do you get entered into a giveaway! You've gotta be at the show to get your prize though. They'll be drawn during the show.
If you can't make it in person, the show will also be streamed online! The stream will be added to the website on the nav bar soon
